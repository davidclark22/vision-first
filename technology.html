Technology

Retinal Camera

The retinal camera documents the inside of the eye in order to detect and manage many pathologies, including Diabetes, Macular Degeneration and Glaucoma. This test allows the doctor to follow any changes from year to year.

Icare ic100 Tonometer

Instead of using the typical "eye puff" machine, we use the Icare tonometer. This instrument allows us to measure eye pressure without a puff of air or drops.

Visual Field

At Vision First, we use the Humphrey Field Analyzer for visual field testing. This instrument is considered the gold standard for visual field testing. Visual fields can detect visual disturbances or loss caused by conditions such as Glaucoma, Macular Degeneration, tumors, retinal detachment, etc.
